Reading Room Rules
Ground rules for every exchange

BookBridge only works if everyone treats it like they're borrowing from a classmate, not a stranger.

  1. 1
    List only what you actually own
    Every listing should be a real book in your hands, ready to hand off.
  2. 2
    Meet on campus, in public
    Handoffs happen in shared spaces — library, canteen, or department block.
  3. 3
    Describe the condition honestly
    Mark it New, Good, Fair, or Worn accurately, and flag any damage up front.
  4. 4
    Respond to requests promptly
    Accept or decline within a few days — don't hold a listing hostage.
  5. 5
    Donations get a quick admin check
    Donated books are reviewed before going live, to keep the shelf genuine.
  6. 6
    No-shows and fake listings get you banned
    Repeated no-shows or bad-faith listings result in account suspension.
